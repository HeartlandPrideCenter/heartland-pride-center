Resource Directory

One place to start when you do not know where to start.

HPC is developing a verified directory of affirming businesses, organizations, providers, and community resources across Polk County.

Directory standards

Trust has to be earned, not assumed.

  • Listings should be LGBTQ+ owned, ally-led, or affirming in practice.
  • Organizations and businesses should consent before being added.
  • Resources should be reviewed for usefulness, respect, and accessibility.
  • The directory should help people find support without feeling exposed or unsafe.

Resource areas

Health

Medical & Prevention

Clinics, testing referrals, PrEP navigation, sexual health education, and wellness providers.

Support

Mental Health

Affirming counselors, peer support options, crisis resources, and community wellness programs.

Stability

Housing & Legal

Organizations and services that help with housing, legal needs, identity documents, and safety planning.

Job readiness, resume support, affirming employers, workforce resources, and training opportunities.

Local

Businesses & Allies

Affirming local businesses, community spaces, service providers, and ally-led organizations.

Community

Events & Groups

Social connection, volunteer groups, Pride events, support circles, and safe community gatherings.

Directory interest

Request to be considered for the affirming resource directory.

This is the first step for businesses, providers, organizations, and community groups interested in being reviewed for future directory inclusion.

Submission does not guarantee listing. HPC will review directory requests before inclusion.
