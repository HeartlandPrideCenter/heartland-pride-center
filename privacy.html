Privacy

Privacy Notice

Heartland Pride Center respects the privacy of people who visit our website, contact us, or use our online forms.

Website Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand general website activity, such as page views, device type, referral sources, and how visitors move through the site. This helps us improve our outreach, resources, and community information.

Forms & Contact

If you submit a form or email us, we may receive the information you choose to provide so we can respond to your message, partnership interest, volunteer inquiry, or resource request.

Sensitive Information

Please avoid sharing highly sensitive personal, medical, legal, or crisis information through general website forms. If you are in immediate danger or need urgent help, use emergency or crisis resources.

Third-Party Tools

Some links may open third-party services such as Google Forms, Givebutter, Facebook, or Instagram. Those services may have their own privacy practices.
