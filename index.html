Why we exist

A home base for people who have had to search too hard for support.

HPC is being built to close the gap between need and access. Our work centers LGBTQ+ people, families, allies, and neighbors who need trustworthy information, affirming connection, and a place that treats dignity as the floor, not the ceiling.

Wellness & Prevention

Education, outreach, safer-sex resources, testing referrals, PrEP navigation, and practical health information.

Resource Navigation

Helping people find affirming support for mental health, housing, legal help, food access, employment, and crisis needs.

Community Connection

Support groups, events, volunteer days, peer connection, and spaces where people can be seen without shrinking.
