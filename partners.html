Business Network

Businesses can join the Land of Hearts.

Local businesses that want to be visible as affirming can apply to join the Heartland Pride Center Affirming Business Network. Approved businesses may appear in the public directory and Land of Hearts map.

Partnership style

Practical help. Clear communication. Real-world impact.

  • Provide referrals or accept referrals from HPC.
  • Host or support workshops, outreach tables, or community events.
  • Sponsor prevention supplies, printing, tents, banners, resource cards, or event materials.
  • Offer mentorship as HPC grows into a stronger prevention and resource organization.
  • Be listed as an affirming resource once verified and approved.
