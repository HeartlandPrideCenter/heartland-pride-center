Partner With Us

Help build the support network Polk County deserves.

HPC is looking for partners, sponsors, mentors, and local allies ready to help build practical LGBTQ+ support across the Heartland.

Health partners

Clinics & Prevention

Testing, PrEP navigation, sexual health education, referrals, outreach training, and public health collaboration.

Community partners

Nonprofits & Agencies

Resource navigation, warm referrals, shared outreach, volunteer coordination, and cross-community support.

Local allies

Businesses & Sponsors

Supply sponsorships, event support, directory participation, printing help, space support, and community visibility.

Partnership style

Practical help. Clear communication. Real-world impact.

  • Provide referrals or accept referrals from HPC.
  • Host or support workshops, outreach tables, or community events.
  • Sponsor prevention supplies, printing, tents, banners, resource cards, or event materials.
  • Offer mentorship as HPC grows into a stronger prevention and resource organization.
  • Be listed as an affirming resource once verified and approved.

Start here

Partnership does not have to be complicated to be meaningful.

Some partners may offer services. Some may offer space, supplies, training, mentorship, sponsorship, or simply a reliable door to knock on. The goal is to build an ecosystem that works for real people in real situations.
