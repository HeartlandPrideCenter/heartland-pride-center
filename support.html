Support Heartland Pride Center

Help build a stronger, safer, more connected Heartland.

Heartland Pride Center is a grassroots nonprofit organization building an LGBTQ+ community resource hub for Polk County and Florida's Heartland. Your support helps turn care into action.

Why support matters

HPC is building practical support from the ground up.

Every contribution helps expand outreach, connect people to affirming resources, support prevention education, strengthen community partnerships, and create visible LGBTQ+ support in a region where access is still too limited.

Give financially

Support the mission through Givebutter.

Financial support helps fund outreach supplies, printing, resource materials, prevention education, technology, community events, and the infrastructure needed to grow Heartland Pride Center responsibly.

What support makes possible

Outreach

Community Presence

Tents, tables, banners, outreach kits, resource cards, event supplies, and materials that help HPC show up in the real world.

Prevention

Health Education

HIV/STI prevention materials, safer-sex resources, testing referral tools, PrEP information, and wellness education.

Connection

Resource Navigation

Technology, printing, directory tools, partner outreach, and systems that help people find affirming help more easily.

More than money

Support can look like time, space, supplies, skills, or partnership.

  • Volunteer for outreach, events, directory research, or community projects.
  • Partner with HPC as a clinic, nonprofit, business, school, agency, or community organization.
  • Sponsor printing, outreach supplies, event materials, prevention resources, or technology.
  • Offer meeting space, training support, mentorship, professional services, or in-kind donations.
  • Share HPC with people who may need resources, connection, or community.

Build with us

Building something meaningful takes a community.

Whether you donate, volunteer, partner, sponsor supplies, share our mission, or help connect someone to support, you are helping create a more connected and affirming Heartland.
