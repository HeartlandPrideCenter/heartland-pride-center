Programs & Services

Support that meets people where they actually are.

HPC is developing practical, community-centered programs focused on prevention, navigation, wellness, and connection.

Prevention

HIV/STI Education

Safer-sex education, prevention information, testing referrals, PrEP navigation, and outreach materials for community events.

Navigation

Resource Guidance

Helping people identify affirming options for mental health, housing, legal support, food access, employment, and other needs.

Belonging

Support & Connection

Future support groups, peer connection, coming-out support, social spaces, and community-building opportunities.

Outreach

Pop-Up Resource Hub

A mobile outreach presence for events, tabling, resource distribution, referrals, and community visibility.

Growth

Life Skills & Workshops

Future workshops around employment, resumes, interviews, financial basics, wellness, advocacy, and community leadership.

Community

Clean With Pride

Service-based volunteer projects that show LGBTQ+ visibility through real contribution, pride, and local care.

Still building

Some services are in development while partnerships, training, and funding are secured.

HPC is early-stage, but the structure is already forming. The goal is to grow responsibly, partner with reputable organizations, and build programs that are useful in real life, not just nice on paper.
